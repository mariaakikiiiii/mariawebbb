Adoption of the Internet

Meanwhile the rates of the use of internet have leveled out after we observed the increasing of it in the middle part of the last decade, so many people are still connecting online for their first time, each month.
The analysis of data from various organizations such as ITU,GSMA Intelligence shows that approximately 97 million people began using the internet last 12 months for their first time and has shown a 1.8 percent year on year growth.
In Lebanon, internet penetration is at 80% of the population as of January 2025, with approximately 5.6 million users.[4]

Internet usage in Lebanon has grown steadily, with speed and adoption metrics showing regional improvements (DataReportal, 2025; Speedtest Global Index, 2025; World Bank, 2023).

Usage of the Internet

The user behavior across the region is influenced by messaging, mobile browsing and social media. Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook and TikTok remain the most popular platforms in Lebanon .

Speed of the Internet

The global speediest index states in Lebanon an average fixed speeds are 50 Mbps almost, and for mobile speeds are around 35 Mbps. While these are still appearing behind global averages it looks like an improvement over the previous years.
